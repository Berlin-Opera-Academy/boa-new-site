Berlin Opera Academy Orchestra empowers emerging artists with real-world experience through immersive summer training program in the world's opera capitol.

A typical day with BOA Orchestra

09:30 - 12:30

Chamber Music, Masterclass or Audition Training

14:00 - 17:00

Opera Repertoire Rehearsal

18:00 - 19:00

German Language Classes

With the goal of introducing performers to Europe's largest music market and providing the necessary skills needed to audition and flourish there, participants will look forward to a four- or eight-week immersive experience.

Summer studies include:
Operatic repertoire training and performance
Chamber music training and performance
German language classes
Audition training
Daily access to practice rooms
Masterclasses and workshops

Early Application Deadline: Wednesday, December 3rd, 2024

Application Instructions: Submit the form below. There is no application fee for orchestral musicians.

Repertoire Requirements: As part of the application form below, upload two recordings that best display your musical abilities. We recommend one excerpt from an operatic or symphonic work and one solo or piano-accompanied piece.
