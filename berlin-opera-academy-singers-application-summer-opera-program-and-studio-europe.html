SUMMER STUDIO SINGERS

Focus on the voice

Over the course of the month, singers will prepare their audition materials to a professional level, complete a role study, audition for agents, and workshop self-promotion strategies to help them get a start in the industry and improve their employment prospects.

Singers will leave Berlin with a German resume, the ability to connect with agents, a video audition recording, and the skills necessary to audition in Germany.
