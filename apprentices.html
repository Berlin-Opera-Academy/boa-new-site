APPRENTICES //

"My apprenticeship at Berlin Opera Academy was a transformative experience which was essential for me in navigating the fast-paced, ever-evolving world of performing arts today. A truly enriching experience!" - Luca M, Italy

BOA Apprentices leave Berlin with the pride of directly contributing to the production of multiple operas.

Apprenticeships and Internships are led by BOA Faculty and Staff across various departments. While being given the autonomy to take on responsibility, embrace opportunities, and work from their own initiative, individuals are able to gain confidence through experience. It is our belief that this is the most effective way of preparing for careers in the opera industry.

Early Application Deadline: Wednesday, December 3rd, 2024

There are no application fees for apprentices and interns.

To apply, submit the form below. Results are sent on a rolling basis.

TUITION & SCHOLARSHIP

Apprentice Conductors: 950€ per session
All other positions: There are no fees for apprentices and interns.

There are a number of partial scholarships available for contributing towards apprentice accommodation costs. Please include scholarship requests in the application form above.

For exceptional apprentices, Berlin Opera Academy supports and facilitates future employment opportunities.

Apprentices are required to submit a refundable tuition deposit of 500€ to hold their position at BOA. This will be refunded upon successful completion of the academy, after the summer ends.
