APPRENTICES //

BOA’s Apprentices are the bedrock of the Academy.

They leave with the pride of directly contributing to the production of multiple operas. Apprentices at BOA will receive personal instruction from industry professionals while learning by doing in their preferred department.

Apprentices are guided by BOA Faculty and Staff while being given the autonomy to take on responsibility, embrace opportunities, and work from their own initiative. We find this is the most effective way of preparing for careers in the opera industry.

TUITION & SCHOLARSHIP


There are no tuition fees for apprentices and interns.

There are a number of partial scholarships available for contributing towards apprentice accommodation costs. Please include scholarship requests in the application form above.

Apprentices are required to submit a refundable tuition deposit of 500€ to hold their position at BOA. This will be refunded upon successful completion of the academy, after the summer ends. In the event of cancellation, early departure or lack of attendance, the deposit becomes non-refundable.

For exceptional apprentices, Berlin Opera Academy supports and facilitates future employment opportunities.
